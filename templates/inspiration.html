Nitrografix

This website uses a font called Monsterrat (https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat). After looking at fonts that various professional websites use I was surprised just how much they influence the feel of the website. It's difficult to explain just how they make me feel, but a bank would use a more vintage feeling font to make you feel like the bank is old, or long standing. As for the font I have chosen to focus on, Monsterrat, I like that it is a plain in just the right way. It is clear and easily readable for conveying information. It is versatile, whether it's used as a header or for readable text in a paragraph.
