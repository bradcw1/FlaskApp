Tripwire Magazine

I was looking for websites that have floating navigation bars, and the website I found detailing them just so happened to have one. I like the idea of a sticky navigation bar so that regardless how deep you get in to a website you can still get home and to major pages. While single page scrolling websites are popular right now I don't think it will work well with the website we are required to create for Assignment 1 as there is minimal scrolling. As it stands on my Inspiration page scrolls and is the only page you will notice that the navigation bar is sticking.
